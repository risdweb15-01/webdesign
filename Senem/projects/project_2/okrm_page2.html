No.2

Before Contingency After the Fact, Gabriel Kuri

2011

Publication to accompany ‘Before Contingency After the Fact’, London’s first solo exhibition of the work of artist Gabriel Kuri held at the South London Gallery. Rather than producing a conventional catalogue, the form of the publication explicitly connects the formal attributes of the artists’ sculptural works to a printed object.

Consisting of a collection of stitched and printed booklets mounted onto a solid case, each part contains an aspect of the content; essay, reference imagery and works. Stickers are applied to the exterior of the case providing a surface for cover information.

Edited by Margot Heller  Photography by Marius Hansen  215 × 280 mm
