No.10

Eclectic Totemic, Haegue Yang

2013

A work created in collaboration with Haegue Yang for ‘Family of Equivocations’ Aubette 1928 and Museum of Modern and Contemporary Art, Strasbourg, France, 2013.

‘Totemic figures are printed on wallpaper; their dimensions are such that they appear to be standing in the room. Laid across three walls, these totemic figurations fill the space and create a jungle of references to modernism, the avant-garde, geometry and other formal languages, which weave associated cultural heritages into the work.’

From the catalogue ‘Family of Equivocations’
