No.9

Le Cabinet de Curiosités de Mlle Clouette

2011

Publication for the peculiar collection of objects and artifacts left behind by the late Shelagh Cluett. Exploring a concept of circularity (front to back or back to front), the publication presents a series of juxtapositions, interrupted at its center by a selection of key specimens and an essay by curator Virginia Whiles.

210 × 280mm, 116 pages, 1000 copies Photography by Nicole Bachmann
