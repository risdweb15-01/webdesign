104 / Le Cent Quatre 2

#05

DATE

January 2008

DESCRIPTION

104 (Le Cent Quatre) is a French cultural institute for which we designed the graphic identity and the sign system. The first part of this assignment was done in 2007. The second part we did in 2008. It is just a small selection. Before anything else, first read 104 / Le Cent Quatre 1; This whole story doesn't make much sense without reading the first part.
One of the first things we did in 2008 was giving a workshop to students. Although 104 was officially not open yet, they already had an educational program running, for which they asked all artists connected to 104 to give workshops to local art academy students.
