DESCRIPTION

‘Graphic Design: Now in Production’ was a group exhibition on the subject of graphic design, curated by Andrew Blauvelt and Ellen Lupton. The exhibition started at the Walker Art Center(Oct 22, 2011 – Jan 22, 2012), then travelled to Governors Island(May 26 – Sep 3, 2012), onwards to the Hammer Museum(Sep 30, 2012 – Jan 6, 2013), and then to the Grand Rapids Art Museum(Feb 1 – Apr 28, 2013).It travelled to the Contemporary Arts Museum in Houston(Jul 20 – Sep 29, 2013), the Southeastern Center for Contemporary Art in Winston-Salem (Oct 18, 2013 – Feb 23, 2014) and RISD( Mar 28 – Aug 10, 2014).
