The Printed Book

#03

DATE

February 2012

DESCRIPTION

‘The Printed Book: A Visual History’ was an exhibition that took place between February 8 and May 13, 2012, at Bijzondere Collecties (the ‘Special Collections’ department of the library of the University of Amsterdam). The exhibition basically consisted of a three-room display of 130 books from the collection of the library of the University of Amsterdam, as selected by curator Mathieu Lommen.
As for ourselves – we were responsible for the design of the exhibition, and for the surrounding printed matter (but not for the design of The Book of Books, which was developed separately from the exhibition).
