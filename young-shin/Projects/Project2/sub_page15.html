Dark Side of Bauhaus

#14

DATE

June 2003

DESCRIPTION

In the mid of 2003, Tokyo art space Rocket Gallery approached us to contribute to 'Jungle LP Show', a group exhibition of record sleeves. Taka Kawachi, the curator of the show, had asked the involved artists and designers to create work inspired by existing record covers. In other words, the work had to be a homage or tribute to an already existing record sleeve. Because of some stressful last-moment antics, we messed up. That's why the composition of the type, especially the lining seems a little sketchy, which is also the reason why this poster isn't our most popular piece. Luckily, we later had a chance to refine the composition, and turn the design into a large-size outdoor poster.
