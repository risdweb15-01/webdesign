DESCRIPTION

In 2012 we were approached by UK writer Alex Coles, who asked us to take care of the graphic design of a new series of annual publications. Titled EP, and published by Sternberg Press, the series would consist of thematically-edited journals/reviews, each issue focusing on one particular subject.
The editorial concept, as it was presented to us, was based on two separate models: the EP (Extended Play) audio-format, and the Brutalist architecture of The Economist Plaza. The following two quotes might clarify the way in which these two models served as an editorial concept.
