NAiM Jean Prouvé

#06

DATE

May 2007

DESCRIPTION

'Jean Prouvé: De Poëzie van het Technisch Object / The Poetics of the Technical Object' was an exhibition that took place at the NAi Maastricht from May 12 to September 2, 2007. Basically, it was a retrospective devoted to the famous French engineer and architect Jean Prouvé.
For NAi Maastricht, we usually design both the printed matter and the sign system of the exhibition. In the case of the Prouvé show, we only had to design the printed matter. Since the exhibition was a travelling Vitra production there was no need for us to design the sign system; the exhibition came completely pre-designed.
